Core Gameplay

Master the Roguelike Deckbuilder Mechanics

⚡ Energy System

  • Base Energy: 3 per turn (replenished at start)
  • Additional Sources: Charms, abilities, and bonuses
  • Strategic Management: Critical to card play timing

Master your energy to unleash devastating combos!

🃏 Deck Building Foundation

  • Starting Deck: 10 basic cards
  • Expansion: Through battles, shops, and events
  • Tonic Slots: 5 available at start
  • Management: Upgrade, add, or remove at key locations

Build synergies and adapt your strategy each run!

⬆️ Card Upgrade System

Upgrade Locations:

  • 🔥 Campfires - Rest and upgrade
  • 🍺 Saloons - Purchase improvements
  • 🎲 Chance encounters - Risk for rewards
  • ⚔️ Victory bonuses - Defeat powerful foes

Enhancement Effects:

  • Reduce energy costs
  • Increase damage output
  • Add powerful new effects

🎮 Single Run Loop (30-60 min)

  1. Select Character & Horse
  2. Navigate Procedural Map
  3. Make Strategic Path Choices
  4. Combat with Card Strategy
  5. Upgrade/Craft at Locations
  6. Face Act Boss
  7. Unlock New Content

Every run is unique with procedural generation!

📊 Character Stats

  • 💪 Strength: Maximum damage output
  • 🛡️ Defense: Damage mitigation
  • 🍀 Luck: Critical hits and encounter outcomes
  • 🎭 Charisma: Shop prices and encounter results
  • ⚡ Agility: Dodge chance and minigame speed

Balance your stats to match your playstyle!
