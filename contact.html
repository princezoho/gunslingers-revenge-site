We're targeting a Q3 2025 release for PC, with console versions following shortly after.

What platforms will the game be available on?

Initially on PC (Steam, Epic Games Store), with PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch versions planned.

Will there be multiplayer?

We're focusing on a solid single-player experience first, but multiplayer modes are under consideration for post-launch.
