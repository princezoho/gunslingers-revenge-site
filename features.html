Game Features

Everything that makes Gunslinger's Revenge unique

Dynamic Combat System

Engage in fast-paced card battles with real-time animations and strategic depth. Chain combos, manage resources, and unleash devastating special moves.

Dual-Wielding Mechanics

Master the art of dual-wielding with unique card combinations. Play two cards simultaneously for powerful synergies and game-changing effects.

Lasso & Capture System

Use your trusty lasso to capture enemies and turn them into allies. Build a posse of converted outlaws to fight alongside you.

Customizable Loadouts

Equip unique accessories and gear that modify your deck's behavior. From lucky horseshoes to enchanted spurs, every item changes your playstyle.

Stunning Visual Effects

Hand-drawn animations and particle effects bring every card to life. Watch as your actions explode across the screen in vibrant Wild West style.

Town Hub System

Visit the general store between runs to upgrade cards, purchase new abilities, and unlock permanent progression rewards.

Core Gameplay Features

  • 100+ unique cards to collect and master
  • 5 distinct character classes with unique playstyles
  • Procedurally generated maps ensure no two runs are alike
  • Boss battles with multiple phases and adaptive AI
  • Daily challenges and leaderboards
  • Cross-save support between platforms
  • New Game+ mode with increased difficulty
  • Endless mode for infinite replayability
