About the Game

Gunslinger's Revenge is a procedurally generated deck‑builder set in the small frontier town of Slingfield. Collect powerful cards, recruit quirky characters and upgrade your deck to survive waves of bandits, bounty hunters and supernatural foes. Every run is different and every choice matters.

Inspired by the original Gunslingers universe, this game brings your favourite little cowpokes to life. Explore the dusty streets, uncover hidden secrets and join events and duels as you fight for fame and fortune.

Features

  • Roguelike progression: no two runs are the same; each playthrough offers new challenges and rewards.
  • Deck‑building strategy: choose your cards wisely to create powerful combos and synergies.
  • Character upgrades: level up your gunslinger, assign stat points and unlock unique abilities.
  • Charming art: hand‑painted Wild West visuals inspired by the original Gunslingers collection.
  • Replayability: meaningful choices, procedural generation and hidden secrets keep you coming back for more.

Visit Slingfield

Discover the heart of Gunslinger’s Revenge: Slingfield, a lively Wild West town where cowpokes gather to trade, spin tall tales and hone their decks. The General Store, inspired by the original Gunslingers microverse art, hides secrets, quests and powerful upgrades waiting to be uncovered.

Upgrade Your Deck

Spend your hard‑earned points on stat boosts and powerful abilities. Within the shaded corners of Slingfield’s saloon you’ll meet mentors who will hone your skills and teach you new tricks.

Make strategic choices on your sheet; every point invested helps your gunslinger stand tall against the toughest foes.
