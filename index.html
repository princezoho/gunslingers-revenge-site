About the Game

Gunslinger's Revenge is a procedurally generated deck‑builder set in the small frontier town of Slingfield. Collect powerful cards, recruit quirky characters and upgrade your deck to survive waves of bandits, bounty hunters and supernatural foes. Every run is different and every choice matters.

Inspired by the Gunslingers NFT universe, this game brings your favourite little cowpokes to life. Explore the dusty streets, uncover hidden secrets and join events and duels as you fight for fame and fortune.

Features

  • Roguelike progression: no two runs are the same; each playthrough offers new challenges and rewards.
  • Deck‑building strategy: choose your cards wisely to create powerful combos and synergies.
  • Character upgrades: level up your gunslinger, assign stat points and unlock unique abilities.
  • Charming art: hand‑painted Wild West visuals inspired by the Gunslingers NFT collection.
  • Replayability: meaningful choices, procedural generation and hidden secrets keep you coming back for more.
