About the Game

Gunslinger’s Revenge is a fast‑paced roguelike deck‑builder where every draw can mean survival or defeat. Assemble a powerful deck, manage your resources and outwit relentless foes in procedurally generated runs. Each adventure presents new combinations, new strategies and new rewards.

With crisp hand‑drawn art and dynamic Wild West combat, Gunslinger’s Revenge delivers a thrilling adventure that never plays the same way twice.

Master the Deck

Choose your cards wisely to build synergies that unleash devastating combos. Adapt to unpredictable encounters, upgrade your gear and tailor your deck to overcome any obstacle.

Your strategy determines your destiny—will you harness the chaos of endless runs or fall to their unforgiving dangers?

Forge Your Legend

Every decision writes your story. Discover secrets, unlock new cards and face off against unpredictable bosses. With each victory you grow stronger, unlocking new paths and possibilities.

Ready to saddle up? Dive into the full story and explore more strategy tips on our blog.
